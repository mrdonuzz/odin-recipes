Asian Coconut Rice

This coconut rice is perfect! I tried many different ways to duplicate my favorite restaurant's coconut rice after they went out of business. Garnish with toasted coconut and chopped cilantro if you like.

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1 ¼ cups water
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 ½ cups uncooked jasmine rice

Steps

  1. Combine coconut milk, water, sugar, and salt in a medium saucepan; stir until sugar is dissolved. Stir in rice.
  2. Bring to a boil over medium heat. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed, 18 to 20 minutes.
